CORRECTED DESIGN

  1. Inductor Package incorrect

    Solution: shoe horned them onto the board 9/18/09

    PCB: Changed the package in the library.

  2. No ground on the spi flash thermal pad

    Solution: none necessary

    PCB: wired it 9/18/09

  3. GND analog and digital from usb to ltc3544

    Solution: Placed short 0 ohm jumper between analog and digital.

  4. Pins 4 and 5 are reversed pin 5 is gnd for usb B

    Solution: short circuit 4 and 5

    PCB: switch pins 5 to gnd and leave pin 4 open(?) 9/18/09

  5. The feedback circuits for 200a and 200b are switched pins one and two

    Solution: Cut the traces to R23 and R24 and hand wired the traces.

    PCB: switch traces to pins one and two on the LTC3544 9/18/09

  6. The VREF on the JTAG chain needs to be wired to 2.5V or VCCAUX

    Solution: Jumped VREF on JTAG to VCCAUX on the voltage regulator LTC3544, bottom side.

    PCB: Wired it 9/18/09

  7. The RDWR pin ,#38, needs to be incorporated into the cpld. (Not done yet)

    Solution: have shorted to grnd pin #39 to pin #38 on fpga. 9/18/09

    PCB: Not done yet

  8. Switchs require a pullup resistor9/18/09

    Solution: Used pullup with the CPLD

    PCB: Wired it.
